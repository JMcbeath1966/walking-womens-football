  45. <h2 class="considerations">Playing - Things to consider!</h2>↩
  46. <p class="considerations">Following the sucess of Englands women football team, womens football is enjoying an exponential uptake for women of all ages joining the game.</p>↩
  47. <p class="considerations">Very different to the regular game of football, walking football caters primarily for the over 40s, although some tournaments are now running for players exclusively in their 60s and 70s.</p>↩
  48. <p class="considerations">Walking football is played at a much slower pace allowing many players who have loved the game throughout their lives to continue playing albeit at a gentler rate, and at the same time encourages players new to the game to take part knowing that the threat of any injury is dramatically reduced.</p>↩
  49. <p class="considerations">We do recommend players wear 'breathable' clothing if possible, but anything they feel comfortable in is fine. A shirt or short designed specifically for sports allows body heat to be expelled and air to circulate better around the body, keeping players cool and preventing overheating.</p>↩
  50. <p class="considerations">It doesn't have to be expensive if you want to invest in kits. The only thing you may need to buy that you don’t already own is a pair of shin pads and perhaps some appropriate footwear.</p> ↩
  51. <p class="considerations">Walking Football can be played in any kind of training shoe, but 'football-specific' trainers will give you more stability and better ball control. The venue may specify what types of footwear are allowed/not allowed. You are advised to contact the club/sports centre before attending.</p> ↩
  52. <p class="considerations">However, if you begin to play Walking Football competitively, you will need to wear the appropriate kit.</p>↩
  53. <p class="considerations">We recommend:</p>↩
  54. <ul>↩
  55. <li class="kit">Surface appropriate trainers or boots</li>↩
  56. <li class="kit">A breathable shirt</li>↩
  57. <li class="kit">Breathable shorts</li>↩
  58. <li class="kit">Shin Pads</li>↩
  59. <li class="kit">Hat, cap and gloves - as appropriate for the weather</li>↩
  60. </ul>↩
